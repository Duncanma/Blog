What’s Wrong With This Code?

Rather [than] bore my audience by presenting my views on implementing IDisposable, I’m going to take the “What’s wrong with this code?” approach. My goal is to present code examples that show code that’s doing something wrong – be it something prohibited by the language, something that’s ill-advised, or something with bad performance – and then let the attendees try to figure out what’s wrong with the code.
