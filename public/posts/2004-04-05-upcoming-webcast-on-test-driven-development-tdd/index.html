Test Driven Development as a Practice – Level 400

Thursday, April 15, 2004, 9:00-10:30 A.M. Pacific Time

Microsoft® Press author Ron Jeffries, author of the recently released “Extreme Programming Adventures in C#” will discuss the application of Extreme Programming design and development techniques using Visual C#® and and the Microsoft .NET Framework. Topics include “Spikes”, unit-testing, and your questions regarding the fundamental techniques of simple design, test-driven development, and refactoring to create practical, .NET-ready applications.
